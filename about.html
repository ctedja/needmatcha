About Us

The world's finest matcha. Straight from Kyoto's tea fields to Sydney's shores.

Handpicked with a focus on only the most premium tea leaves, our ceremonial-grade matcha is designed to offer a sprinkle of perfection. We also transform these into delightful drinks for all to enjoy at markets across Sydney.

Frequently Asked Questions

Ceremonial grade is the highest quality matcha, made from the youngest tea leaves with stems and veins removed. It's stone-ground into a fine powder with a vibrant green color, smooth texture, and naturally sweet, umami-rich taste — perfect for drinking on its own or in lattes.

Our matcha comes directly from Kyoto, Japan — the birthplace of matcha production. We partner with small, family-run farms that have been growing tea for generations.
